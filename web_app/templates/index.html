Welcome to the OpenSuse Human Population Genetics Web Application

This is a platform where you can perform population genetic structure analysis on the integrated Eurasia+1000 Genomes project data of chromosome 1 for almost 4000 individuals. This portal allows you to understand how Eurasian samples relate to other human populations in their genetics by means of population structure analysis with clustering, admixture analysis as well as comparing genotype and allele frequencies in genomic regions, genes, or SNPs of interest. Happy Researching!
